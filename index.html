Part of the Faculty of Computing and Information Technology (FOCIT)

Welcome to IT Department

The Information Technology Department at our university is a vibrant community of scholars, researchers, and students dedicated to advancing the field of IT through education, innovation, and research.

We offer comprehensive programs that prepare students for careers in various IT domains, equipping them with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to excel in the rapidly evolving tech landscape.
